# Privacy Policy - Night Clock

**Last updated:** April 3, 2026

## Overview

Night Clock is a simple bedside clock application. We take your privacy seriously.

## Data Collection

Night Clock does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app:

- Does not require an internet connection
- Does not collect analytics or usage data
- Does not use advertising or tracking SDKs
- Does not access contacts, location, camera, or microphone
- Does not share any data with third parties

## Local Storage

The app stores your preferences (theme color, brightness, 12h/24h format) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

## Battery Status

The app reads your device's charging status solely to enable the auto-launch feature when the charger is connected. This information is not stored or transmitted.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: nightclock@pccosta.com

## Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
